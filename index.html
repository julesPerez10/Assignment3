Bon Bon Bum

Bon Bon Bum is a Colombian popsicle brand known for its wide variety of flavors and long-lasting popularity in the country.

Ingredients: Bon Bon Bum typically contain ingredients such as sugar, glucose syrup, water, citric acid, natural and artificial flavors, food coloring, malic acid, salt, gum arabic, and preservatives such as potassium sorbate. These ingredients are combined to create the different varieties of flavors offered by this Colombian popsicle brand.
